FastPunch Pro Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

Introduction

FastPunch Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, company name (when you create an account via Firebase Auth or Sign in with Apple)
  • Punch List Data: Voice recordings, photos, notes, project details, lot numbers, trade information
  • Device Information: Device type, iOS version (for app functionality)

Optional AI Training Data (Pro Tier)

  • Voice Patterns: Anonymized voice input text for improving speech recognition (opt-in only)
  • What We DON'T Collect: Your actual voice recordings, photos, names, addresses, or any personally identifiable information
  • Opt-in Required: You must explicitly enable this in Settings. Default is OFF.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: App crashes, feature usage (to improve the app)
  • Location Data: Optional GPS coordinates for punch items (only if you enable)
  • Photos: Images you attach to punch list items

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the FastPunch Pro service
  • Sync your data across devices via Firebase
  • Generate exports (PDF, CSV) of your punch lists
  • Improve voice recognition accuracy
  • Send you app updates and support communications
  • Analyze app performance and fix bugs

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your data is stored on your device using CoreData
  • Cloud Storage: We use Firebase (Google Cloud) for secure cloud sync, authentication, and photo storage
  • Firestore Database: Your project and punch list data is stored in Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Storage: Photos are stored in Firebase Cloud Storage with encryption
  • Security: We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest

Web Sharing Feature

FastPunch Pro allows you to generate shareable web links for your punch lists:

  • Public Links: When you generate a share link, anyone with the URL can view that specific punch list
  • No Authentication Required: Trade contractors can view shared reports without creating an account
  • 30-Day Expiration: Shared links automatically expire after 30 days and are deleted from our servers
  • Photo Access: If you include photos in your shared report, those photos are accessible via the public link
  • Read-Only: Viewers cannot modify or delete your data through share links

You control what is shared: Only items you explicitly choose to export are included in share links.

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your punch list data with other users (unless you explicitly share a project)
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Access your photos or voice recordings without your permission

We DO share data with:

  • Firebase/Google Cloud: For secure cloud storage and authentication
  • Apple: For App Store distribution and TestFlight (if applicable)
  • Law Enforcement: Only if required by law or court order

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Export all your data anytime (PDF, CSV)
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Modify: Edit or delete any punch list item
  • Offline Use: Use the app without cloud sync (data stays local)

Voice Recordings and Photos

  • Voice: Transcribed on-device using Apple's Speech framework. Audio recordings are not stored permanently unless you choose to keep them.
  • Photos: You control which photos are attached to punch items. We do not access your camera roll without permission.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, storage
  • Apple Sign In: Authentication option

Children's Privacy

FastPunch Pro is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
